Who We Are

Founded by Mwiseneza Patrick & Patrick Mbonimpa On July 14th, 2019, we founded LA CAVE DE PAPE fuelled by a deep-rooted passion for extraordinary wines and champagnes. The journey began with a shared vision among a group of like-minded wine enthusiasts, and together, we set out to redefine the online shopping experience for premium beverages. Over the years, LA CAVE DE PAPE has evolved to offer more than just wines and champagnes. Our catalog now includes an extensive range of gins, vodkas, rums, tequilas, liqueurs, and more, catering to a diverse audience with varying tastes. Efficiency is key at LA CAVE DE PAPE. We ensure prompt and secure delivery services to our customers. Our online store provides a 100% secure checkout process, with various payment methods, including MTN Mobile Money, VISA (3D Secure), and traditional options like cash and card payments on delivery. As we continue to steer LA CAVE DE PAPE into the future, our commitment to quality, rarity, and exceptional customer service remains unwavering. We're excited about shaping the future of the fine beverage industry and connecting enthusiasts with the world's most sought-after libations.
