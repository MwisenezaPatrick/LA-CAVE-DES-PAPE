Product Details


Veuve Clicquot Brut is a brand of champagne produced by the Champagne house Veuve Clicquot in Reims, France. The wine is made from a blend of Chardonnay, Pinot Noir, and Pinot Meunier grape varieties, and is noted for its crisp, dry flavor and delicate bubbles. Aromas and flavors include citrus, green apple, and toast. Often enjoyed as an aperitif or paired with seafood or white meat dishes.
